Nanohalo coiled-coil histone

Nanohalo coiled-coil histones are coiled-coiled-like histones from Nanohaloarchaea. Like coiled-coil (CC) histones, Nano CC histones have a C-terminal Î±-helix which is predicted to facilitate tetramerization. We put the CC histones from Nanohaloarchaea in a separate category because they share <20% sequence identity with CC histones.
