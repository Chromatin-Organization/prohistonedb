Viral triplet

Viral triplets are pseudotrimeric histones from megaviruses, encoding three histone folds per monomer. Generally, viral triplets encode eukaryotic-like H2A+H2B and either H3 or H4, or H3+H4 and either H2A or H2B together into one monomeric protein. They likely form nucleosome structures similar to viral singlets and doublets.
