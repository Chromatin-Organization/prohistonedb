Sequence coverage &
local confidence (LDDT)

The predicted local difference distance test (pLDDT) is a measure of AlphaFold2's local confidence in the secondary structure within domains.

A pLDDT value is assigned to every residue and ranges from 0 to 100, with 100 being very high confidence.
The pLDDT values should not be used to evaluate the relative position of domains.
The AlphaFold2 structures are colored by their pLDDT values.

Low pLDDT values (<50) are generally viewed as a prediction of disorder.
