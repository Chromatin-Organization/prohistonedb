Rab GTPase histone

Rab GTPase histones are part of the a3 superfamily as they contain a truncated a3 helix. They are exclusive to Lokiarchaeota. Rab GTPase histones have a fold-to-fold-like histone domain on their C-terminus and a eukaryotic-like small Rab GTPase domain on their N-terminus. The function of these histones is unknown.
